Hello 

I study how strategic reasoning and cognitive abilities develop across the life cycle.

I’m a PhD candidate in Data Science (Behavioral Economics) at Loyola Andalucía University. I use lab-in-the-field experiments to understand how strategic coordination, decision-making and cognitive abilities evolve — especially in children and teenagers. My interest in improving welfare has led me to several professional experiences as a poverty specialist and public policy analyst. I have also worked extensively as a field coordinator, and I’m open to freelance opportunities coordinating, running and organizing field research and experiments. You’ll find my publications and CV below, or you can get in touch.
